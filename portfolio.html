HighFi WiFi check it out here
2014
One of the most amazing places I have ever been to that encouraged makers and collaboration was the FabLab London. It was a huge space that gave everyone access to 3D Printers, CNC Machines and lots of resources. I had been here previously with my brother to do some 3D Printing, however I was here for an event when I was a part of creating this HighFi device. At the hackathon, I was in a team that wanted to provide rural areas, that had been recently struck by a natural disaster, or otherwise, with WiFi coverage to increase the communication with others around the world. By providing these places with WiFi, locals could contact their family and let them know they are okay. This could also have been used a way to minimise the need for Search and Discover because you could monitor how many devices were connected to a specific network and the idendity of these locals.
