Revolutionary cat play device?

I always wanted to build a toy for my cat, Maya, but never had the chance and the resources for the kind I was thinking of. I found that my cat loves playing with lasers, as I am sure other cats do. I strapped a laser powered by an Arduino to the top of my RC Ferrari car. Maya chased the laser around the house while I was having fun playing with the RC car.
